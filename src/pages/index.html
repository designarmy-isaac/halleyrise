Innovation

Rising above the Dulles Toll Road, Halley Rise brings over 1.5 million square feet of state-of-the-art office space to Reston, creating a sense of place that augments the traditional work environment. Brookfield’s commitment to excellence is evident in the contemporary design of each building and overall site plan with anchor retail and public space activations. Located in the heart of Reston, on the newly constructed Silver Line and minutes from Dulles International Airport, Halley Rise provides office tenants with an innovative location and accessibility unrivaled in the area.

INNOVATION

Entertainment

Halley Rise brings entertainment options that elevate everyday life by maintaining a uniquely welcoming and helpful environment for visitors, residents and employees. Year-round art installations and concerts curated by Arts Brookfield alongside a modern movie theater and 250K square feet of premier retail compliment the experiential attributes of the neighborhood full of dynamic energy and a touch of “zen”.

Community

Alive with social activity and ever-changing experiences, Halley Rise will be fine-tuned to the sensibilities of the urban creative class, giving the entire Reston area a platform to discover what’s happening now in global and local culture. Halley Rise is a neighborhood that favors quality of life over quantity of things; a place where you can live, work and play like you’re in downtown without the hassle. Walking distance to the Metro, bike paths and over 5 acres of expansive public parks for the entire community to enjoy, Halley Rise is Reston's new landmark destination.
