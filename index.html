Where Business Meets Pleasure

Halley Rise transcends the traditional work environment with world-class design, sleek architecture and state-of-the-art public spaces. Located on the newly constructed Silver Line and minutes from Dulles International Airport, the neighborhood features 1.5 million square feet of innovative office space. Halley Rise gives workers an iconic location and accessibility unrivaled in the area. It’s a place to get creative, get inspired and get down to business.

Where Entertainment Gets a Touch of Zen

Halley Rise offers entertainment options for everyone—from the urban chic to local families. A modern movie theater and a variety of coveted restaurants highlight the 250,000 square feet of retail. Halley Rise is alive with year-round art installations and concerts in the park curated by Arts Brookfield, creating a hub of social and cultural activity. Innovative architecture weaves light, calming space into the everyday bustle. It’s a place to be seen—and be serene.

Where the Community Comes Together

Welcome to a neighborhood that favors quality of life over quantity of things. It’s where you can live, mingle and play like you’re downtown but without the hassle. Featuring 1,500 new homes walking distance to the Metro, the neighborhood offers bike paths and over five acres of expansive public parks for the entire community to enjoy. With a uniquely welcoming environment for residents, every detail is tailored to the sensibilities of adventurous city dwellers. It’s a place to connect—and unwind.
